Discover Nigerian Fashion

An Unforgettable Cultural and Creative Journey

About the Program

Experience the rich traditions and modern innovations of Nigerian fashion. From iconic fabrics like Ankara and Adire to exclusive fashion shows and hands-on workshops, immerse yourself in the heartbeat of Nigerian creativity.

Program Highlights

  • Guided tours of textile markets and design studios
  • Interactive workshops: Fabric dyeing, gele tying, and jewelry making
  • Exclusive fashion shows featuring top designers
  • Cultural immersion with Nigerian cuisine, music, and dance

Sample Itinerary

Enjoy a carefully curated 7-day journey:

  • Day 1: Arrival and Welcome Reception
  • Day 2: Textile Market Tour
  • Day 3: Interactive Fabric Dyeing Workshop
  • Day 4: Designer Studio Visits
  • Day 5: Fashion Show and Networking
  • Day 6: Jewelry Making and Cultural Immersion
  • Day 7: Farewell Dinner
